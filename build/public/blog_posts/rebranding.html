We Rebranded!

Tres Studios is now Pixel Planet! As we launch on this new adventure, we are honored to have you by our side. We couldn't be more excited to celebrate this significant milestone with you, as you have been an integral part of our success.

We cherish the relationships we have built under the name Tres Studios, but we believe that our new identity as Pixel Planet better reflects our vision, creativity, and passion for innovative and human-centered technology.

About Pixel Planet

At Pixel Planet, we humanize tech. We firmly believe that technology should be a tool, not a tyrant. Our work is driven by the belief that Pixels are for the People. We create solutions that challenge us to be our best and cater to our better human nature. We Defy Dystopia.

Our mission is to Unify Understanding. Through technical innovation, we connect people in a digital world. We strongly believe that creating understanding is what unites us across the pixels of our screens and the world.

Stay In Touch!

Stay tuned for more updates and opportunities as we unveil our new brand identity. Thank you for being part of our story, and we cannot wait to embark on this new chapter with you!
