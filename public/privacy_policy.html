Privacy Policy and Disclaimer

We started Pixel Planet because we were tired of the greedy and corrupt practices in tech. We don’t approve of how companies hang free services in front of users like a carrot - in order to turn users and their data into commodities to be sold to external companies with questionable ethics.

We operate under the principle of people over profits and respect our visitors’ right to privacy. We do use basic analytics in order to see basic anonymous info about where visitors to our site are coming from and the search terms they used to find us. But this is only to ensure we are able to serve and reach our audience better.

We do not use trackers to track users across the web, we do not use deceptive practices to identify visitors in order to tailor targeted ads to them (we don’t even show ads on our site). And if we ever include affiliate links anywhere in our blog posts or website, they will always be clearly marked and identified as affiliate links and for our users’ benefit.

Any information users submit via our contact form or email is treated as confidential and we never sell leads or visitor data to anyone.

We never, in no uncertain terms, sell your data to any third party services for profit. Ever. And we never will. Our only goal in using basic analytics is to help grow our business in an ethical manner and reach audiences who may find our services helpful.

Pixel Planet welcomes your feedback and you can direct any questions or concerns you may have to us via our contact form.
